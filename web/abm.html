Accountancy, Business & Management





This is an introductory course in accounting, business, and management data analysis that will develop students’ appreciation of accounting as a language of business and an understanding of basic accounting concepts and principles that will help them analyze business transactions.


The ABM Track is for those who plan on taking up Economics, Business Administration, Accountancy and Marketing in college.


The ABM strand is perfect for those inclined in mathematics and for those persons who want to venture into the world of business, marketing and enterprise.


ABM is one of the best offer that Pasay City South High School could give. We have instructors that are both licensed CPA's & a professor.


The students who undergo immersion in Sky Freight an Accounting firm got great compliments for a job well done.
